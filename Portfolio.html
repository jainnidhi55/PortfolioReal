Jackson Pollick Painting Project

In this project, I used Javascript to create a Jackson Pollick painting. It creates a style similar to a paint brush, resulting in a large circle and then a series of smaller and smaller circles. The colors of the paint are randomized between brushes, but I have set them so that they are within the same scheme. Using this program, you can create several unique pieces of art!

Aquarium

Here, I created a graphic aquarium which included three distinct types of fish. The goldfish followed the mouse, the large, triangular fish would go back and forth inside the tank, and the shell near the sand would open and close its mouth, randomly secreting bubbles.

City Scroller

In this project, I created a game in which the background city moves throughout the game. The scene takes place in a starry night with a full moon and a shooting star. As the main player jumps over the evil rabbits, the buildings of random heights move past the screen. This was a particularly challenging project, as it is difficult to store all of the buildings without causing a stack overflow. As a solution, I decided to delete a building everytime one is added.

Brick Breaker Paddle Ball

Here, I made a typical paddle ball game in which the goal is to keep the ball bouncing on the paddle.I also added bricks and a point system, the more bricks one hits, the more points accumulate. If the player loses and drops the ball, he has 4 lives to keep playing.
